Technology partners

Intel is the leading maker of computer processors in the world. Proprio is proud to have Intel as an investor and technology partner, supporting our industry-leading work in depth sensing and immersive image processing.
HTC is a pioneer in virtual reality. Proprio is proud to have HTC as an investor in the company, and a partner in applying real-time, immersive light field imaging for VR.
NVIDIA is the world leader in graphics processing. Proprio proudly partners with NVDIA on groundbreaking graphics streaming and AR/VR visualization.
Varjo is building the world's first human eye resolution display for VR. Proprio is working with Varjo to apply this innovative technology to the most compelling use case in the world: brain surgery.
The University of Washington Allen School of Computer Science is among the best CS programs in the world. Proprio's founding team comes from UW and we continue to draw on the vast talent and research capabilities on the Seattle campus.
Samsung is one of the most successful electronics companies on the market today. Proprio gladly leverages a range of Samsung's technologies and expertise, from head-mounted displays to solid state memory.

Clinical research partners

Faculty at Seattle Children's Hospital have been instrumental in validating Proprio's imaging systems across many surgical fields, including neurosurgery, orthopedic, and general surgery.
The University of Washington Department of Neurological Surgery is world class. Proprio is working with UW surgeons to validate and test our systems from the benchtop to the cadaver lab and beyond.
