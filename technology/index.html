A revolutionary light field capture and rendering system

Our proprietary system is built around networked camera arrays, depth-sensing technology and powerful computer graphics processing.

When we can see more clearly, we magnify all of our capacities.

By synthesizing views from multiple sources—including several real-time video feeds—we enable users to navigate live 3D environments with an enhanced sense of presence and accurate depth of field from any perspective.
In this fluid and intuitive computer-mediated reality, users can see and manipulate objects and spaces with groundbreaking levels of cognitive acuity and agility and unprecedented sensory dexterity.

Proprio is leveraging machine intelligence to augment and enhance human capabilities.

The human anatomy, with its complexity and dynamism is one of the richest and most valuable data sets in the world. We believe that the physician-patient interaction is equally valuable.

Proprio will capture and combine the two in order to better comprehend this multi-layered complexity and provide a set of rich insights to enhance the practice of medicine well into the future.
Today, Proprio is leveraging ML to improve the real-time capture and render of immersive live volumetric video of the real world. Think of it as a hyper-accurate 3D canvas of everything.
‍
We are training neural networks to enhance visualizations to see around corners, layer on additional data sets, and view the world as never before.
